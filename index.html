Why readers come here

Signal first. Then the claw marks.

Dr. Mews does not care about fluffy launch language, strategic vapor, or executives saying “metaverse” like it pays rent. The job here is simple: isolate the hard facts, run them through the lab, and tell you whether you’re looking at real category motion or a cardboard toy with a fancy box.
